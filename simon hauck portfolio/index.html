Simon is a UX designer and artist, based in Vancouver.

Simon’s goal is to reduce user frustration through thoughtful design. He believes that products should be enjoyable to use, and provide joy to those who interact with them.

Guided by curiosity, experimentation, and a commitment to continuous learning, Simon approaches design with both a practical mindset and an urge to discover.

Simon earned his Bachelor of Fine Arts from Emily Carr University and is currently pursuing a Diploma in Interaction Design from Capilano University.
